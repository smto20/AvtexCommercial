In my opinion, the simple way is always the best. Just take your peanut butter, jelly, and bread and follow these steps:

  1. Spread peanut butter on both pieces of bread (no one wants the bread getting soggy from jelly!)
  2. Take jelly and spread it over the peanut butter on one piece
  3. Put the pieces together and enjoy!
