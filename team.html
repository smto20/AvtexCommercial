Who We Are

AVTEX Commercial Properties is headquartered in Greenville, SC with an office in Mt. Pleasant, SC and focuses on real estate development and investments. We develop, own, and manage real estate properties in the southeastern US.

Our diverse portfolio includes retail shopping centers, business parks, industrial centers, medical and office building facilities, and mixed-use developments.
